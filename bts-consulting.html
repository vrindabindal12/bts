What We Do

Every engagement is scoped, outcome-focused, and calibrated for small and mid-market professional services firms.

Analytics Consulting

We design analytics architectures that give decision-makers the right information, in the right format, at the right time. From KPI frameworks to full reporting ecosystems.

Power BI Development

Custom Power BI dashboards and reports built for professional services firms that need financial, operational, and client-level visibility in one environment.

Python & Data Engineering

Data pipelines, extraction, transformation, and automation using Python. We eliminate manual reporting work and improve data reliability across your operations.

Tableau Visualization

Interactive, high-clarity Tableau visualizations for both operational management and client-facing reporting. Clean, purposeful, and maintainable.

AI-Enabled Consulting

We help firms identify where AI genuinely adds value — and where it doesn't. Tooling evaluation, prompt design, process integration, ROI framing. Grounded in judgment, not hype.

Decision Support & Automation

Automated reporting, structured decision frameworks, and workflow automation — helping teams move faster with better information, without adding complexity.

How We Engage

Three-Phase Engagement Model

01

Diagnose

We assess your data environment and reporting gaps. No assumption that a full rebuild is needed — we start with what you have and identify the highest-value opportunities.

02

Design

We produce a clear, scoped plan — tools, timeline, deliverables, expected outcomes. You approve before we build. No scope creep, no surprise deliverables.

03

Deliver

We implement, test, and hand off work your team can actually manage. We build capability — not dependency. You own the output.
