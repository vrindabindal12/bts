Recommendation Summary

Atlas — Recommended

Option A: Copper & Teal on Navy

Retains BTS family identity. Copper's value signal is ideal for a billing product. Best brand coherence across the BTS ecosystem. Switch to Option B if you want stronger SaaS signal or Option C if your buyers prefer light mode interfaces.

Sentry — Recommended

Option A: Teal & Copper on Navy

Swapping teal to primary for Sentry creates clear product differentiation from Atlas while staying within the BTS family. Switch to Option B for maximum security/enterprise positioning or Option C if targeting senior lawyers who work in traditional firm environments.

To implement a different theme option: update the CSS custom properties in shared.css — specifically the --cu, --tl, and --bg variables. Product sites can override these with a <style> block after the shared stylesheet import.
