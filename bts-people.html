Chief Technology Officer — BTS

Manan Bedi brings over five years of experience at the intersection of data science, product analytics, and applied AI. Before joining BTS, he served as Analytics Team Lead at Paytm — one of India's largest fintech platforms — where he led data analysis, business intelligence, and automation initiatives across a portfolio managing billions in transaction volume.

His work spanned performance tracking, predictive modelling, PnL reporting, and the development of Python-based automation that materially improved the speed and reliability of operational reporting at scale. His technical grounding is broad and applied: Python, SQL, Power BI, Machine Learning, and Generative AI — deployed on real problems, not academic credentials alone.

Manan has mentored and judged at over ten hackathons including NASA Space Apps, hackCBS, and HackTheMountain, and has published peer-reviewed research on computational approaches to public health — evidence of a mind that moves between technical rigor and real-world application.
