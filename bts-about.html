About BTS

Business Clarity.
Technology Precision.
Delivered Together.

BTS was founded on a straightforward premise: the best technology for professional services firms is not the most sophisticated — it is the technology that makes the business simpler, more profitable, and easier to manage.

Our Mission

Why BTS Exists

We operate at the intersection of business understanding and technology capability. Our product division designs and maintains software built specifically for legal, accounting, and consulting practices. Our consulting division helps small and mid-market firms harness analytics, automation, and AI in practical, commercially sound ways.

Our clients are not looking for another vendor. They are looking for a partner with the judgment to know when to build, when to advise, and when to say something is not the right solution. That is the standard we hold ourselves to.

"BTS exists to make professional services firms more precise, more profitable, and more accountable — through technology that genuinely understands the business of professional services."

Our Approach

Every engagement begins with a question we take seriously: what does this firm actually need? Not what is technically possible. Not what the current trend recommends. What does the specific business problem require?
