Core Capabilities

What Sentry Does

Structured Comparison

Consistent, predefined methodology applied to every document pair. Not a different prompt each time — the same rigour, every run.

Risk-Flag Engine

Material clause changes surfaced first. Jurisdiction, term length, liability caps — flagged by significance, not buried in tracked changes.

Secure Processing

Documents processed in an isolated, controlled environment. Not used for model training. Appropriate handling for NDA-level confidentiality.

Audit Trail

Every comparison logged with unique reference ID, timestamp, and user attribution. Searchable, exportable, and permanently available.

Faster Review Cycles

What used to take an hour of side-by-side reading takes minutes in Sentry. Faster cycles mean faster deal closings and client responses.

Multi-Party Tracking

Track change history across versions and counterparties through multiple rounds of markup and return.

Why Not Generic AI?

Four Reasons Generic AI Tools
Fall Short for Legal Document Review

🔒

Security

Pasting an NDA into a consumer chatbot is a data governance problem. Sentry processes sensitive documents in a controlled environment with appropriate security architecture.

≡

Consistency

A chatbot response depends on how the prompt was written that day. Sentry applies the same methodology every time — regardless of who runs it or when.

📋

Traceability

A chatbot conversation is not a legal record. Sentry maintains a permanent, immutable log of every comparison event — user, document, timestamp, output.

Individual AI subscriptions across a legal team add up with no visibility into total spend. Sentry provides a managed environment at predictable per-comparison pricing.
